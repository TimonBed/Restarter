# Safety Instructions - PC Restarter

**Please read these safety instructions carefully before operating the device. Keep this document for future reference.**

---

## ⚠️ General Safety Instructions

### Intended Use

The PC Restarter is exclusively intended for:

- Remote control of desktop computers via the power button connection
- Monitoring PC operating status via the power LED
- Use in dry indoor environments
- Use by persons with appropriate technical knowledge

**Any other use is considered improper and may result in damage or injury.**

---

## ⚡ Electrical Safety

### Power Supply

- ✅ Only operate with 5V DC USB power supply
- ✅ Only use USB power adapters with CE marking
- ✅ Max. current: 1A
- ❌ Do not use damaged cables or power adapters
- ❌ Do not connect to high voltage

### Before Installation

- ⚠️ **Completely shut down PC**
- ⚠️ **Unplug PC power cable**
- ⚠️ **Wait a few minutes for residual charge to dissipate**

### Electrostatic Discharge (ESD)

- Ground yourself before working on PC (e.g., touch radiator pipe)
- Handle electronic components by edges only
- Anti-static wrist strap recommended

---

## 🔥 Fire Prevention

- Do not cover the device
- Ensure adequate ventilation
- Do not place near flammable materials
- Do not place near heat sources
- If smoke or unusual odor occurs, disconnect power immediately

---

## 💧 Moisture Protection

- ❌ Do not use in humid rooms (bathroom, kitchen, basement)
- ❌ Protect from splashing water and condensation
- ❌ Do not operate with wet hands
- ❌ Do not store liquids near the device

**Protection Rating: IP20 (no protection against water)**

---

| Parameter | Range |
|-----------|-------|

---

## 🔧 Installation and Maintenance

### Installation

- Installation only by persons with technical knowledge
- Do not open or modify the device
- Only use supplied or compatible cables
- Pay attention to correct connection (observe polarity)

### Maintenance

- **No user-serviceable parts inside**
- Do not use if damaged
- Clean only with dry, lint-free cloth
- Do not use cleaning agents or solvents

### Repair

- Repairs only by manufacturer or authorized service centers
- If defective: disconnect power and contact support

---

## 👶 Children and Vulnerable Persons

- Keep device out of reach of children
- Small parts can be swallowed (choking hazard)
- Not suitable as a toy
- Installation by adults only

---

## 📡 Radio Safety

The device contains a WiFi transmitter (2.4 GHz):

- Complies with electromagnetic field exposure limits
- Do not use near medical devices
- With pacemaker: maintain minimum distance of 15 cm (6 inches)
- On aircraft: switch off device

## 🆘 In Emergency

### In Case of Malfunction

1. Disconnect device from power immediately
2. Do not continue to use
3. Contact support

### In Case of Fire Hazard

1. Disconnect device from power (if safe to do so)
2. Leave the room
3. Call fire department (112 in EU / 911 in US)
4. Never extinguish with water

### In Case of Electric Shock

1. Interrupt power supply
2. Provide first aid
3. Call emergency services (112 in EU / 911 in US)
